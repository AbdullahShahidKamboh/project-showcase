# SEO Intelligence Platform

> A comprehensive SEO and digital marketing intelligence platform built with Laravel, designed to automate business discovery, backlink analysis, Google data collection, social media intelligence, and reporting through a centralized dashboard.

---

# Overview

The SEO Intelligence Platform is a powerful web application that streamlines SEO research and digital marketing workflows by aggregating data from multiple online sources into a single dashboard.

The platform enables users to discover businesses, analyze backlink profiles, monitor Google search data, track social media presence, and generate actionable SEO reports, helping marketing teams make data-driven decisions.

✅ **Completed**

# Business Goal

The goal of the platform was to build an all-in-one SEO intelligence system capable of:

- Discovering businesses from multiple sources
- Collecting SEO metrics automatically
- Monitoring backlink profiles
- Aggregating Google business data
- Tracking social media presence
- Generating comprehensive reports
- Improving digital marketing workflows

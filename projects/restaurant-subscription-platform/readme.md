# 🍽️ Restaurant Subscription Platform

> A full-stack restaurant subscription and membership management platform built with Laravel, featuring subscription plans, online payments, customer dashboards, restaurant management, and a powerful administration panel.

---

# Overview

The Restaurant Subscription Platform is designed to help restaurants offer recurring subscription-based services while providing customers with a seamless experience for discovering restaurants, purchasing memberships, and managing their subscriptions.

The platform centralizes restaurant management, subscription billing, customer engagement, and analytics into a single scalable system.

# Business Goal

The goal of the platform was to create a subscription-driven ecosystem capable of:

- Increasing customer retention
- Simplifying membership management
- Automating subscription workflows
- Providing restaurants with recurring revenue streams
- Delivering a modern user experience
- Centralizing restaurant and subscription administration
